WHAT IS MATLAB?

MATLAB (MATRIX LABORATORY) IS A HIGH-LEVEL PROGRAMMING LANGUAGE

AND INTERACTIVE ENVIRONMENT USED FOR NUMERICAL COMPUTING, DATA

ANALYSIS, ALGORITHM DEVELOPMENT, MODELING, AND SIMULATION. IT WAS

DEVELOPED BY MATHWORKS AND IS WIDELY USED BY ENGINEERS,

SCIENTISTS, AND RESEARCHERS FOR TECHNICAL COMPUTING.

 

WHY IS MATLAB IMPORTANT?

• ✅EASY TO LEARN – MATLAB HAS A SIMPLE AND INTUITIVE SYNTAX.

✅POWERFUL COMPUTATION – IT CAN EFFICIENTLY SOLVE LARGE MATHEMATICAL

AND ENGINEERING PROBLEMS.

✅VISUALIZATION TOOLS – IT PROVIDES BUILT-IN FUNCTIONS FOR GRAPHING AND

DATA VISUALIZATION.

✅TOOLBOXES – IT HAS SPECIALIZED TOOLBOXES FOR MACHINE LEARNING, DEEP

LEARNING, CONTROL SYSTEMS, IMAGE PROCESSING, AND SIGNAL PROCESSING.

✅SIMULATION & MODELING – MATLAB, ALONG WITH SIMULINK, IS IDEAL FOR

REAL-WORLD SYSTEM MODELING AND SIMULATION.

 

MAIN USES OF MATLAB

• 1️NUMERICAL COMPUTING

• SOLVING LINEAR ALGEBRA PROBLEMS (MATRICES, VECTORS)

• DIFFERENTIAL EQUATIONS AND MATHEMATICAL MODELING

• STATISTICAL ANALYSIS AND DATA PROCESSING

 

MODELING & SIMULATION

• SIMULINK IS USED TO MODEL AND SIMULATE REAL-WORLD SYSTEMS

• CONTROL SYSTEMS AND ROBOTICS SIMULATIONS

• ELECTRICAL CIRCUIT MODELING AND TESTING

 

ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE & MACHINE LEARNING

• NEURAL NETWORKS AND DEEP LEARNING MODELS

• REGRESSION, CLASSIFICATION, CLUSTERING

CONTROL SYSTEMS & ROBOTICS

• PID CONTROLLERS, KALMAN FILTERS

• AUTONOMOUS VEHICLES AND DRONES

• ROBOTIC ARM KINEMATICS

 

HOW CAN YOU USE MATLAB IN MODELING &

SIMULATION?

• SINCE YOU TEACH MODELING AND SIMULATION, YOU CAN USE MATLAB AND

SIMULINK TO CREATE:

✔PHYSICS SIMULATIONS (PROJECTILE MOTION, PENDULUM, FLUID DYNAMICS)

✔CONTROL SYSTEM MODELING (PID CONTROLLERS, STATE-SPACE MODELS)

✔AI & MACHINE LEARNING APPLICATIONS (PREDICTIVE MODELS)

✔WIRELESS NETWORK SIMULATIONS (IOT, 5G, AND AI-BASED NETWORK

MODELS)

✔MECHANICAL & ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING SIMULATIONS (POWER

SYSTEMS, HEAT TRANSFER)
